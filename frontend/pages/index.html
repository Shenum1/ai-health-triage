Four levels of care guidance

The system assesses your symptoms and assigns one of four triage levels.

L1

Self-Care

Minor symptoms you can manage at home with rest and over-the-counter remedies.

L2

See a Doctor

Symptoms warranting a GP appointment within 24 to 72 hours for professional evaluation.

L3

Urgent Care

Symptoms requiring same-day medical attention at an urgent care centre or walk-in clinic.

L4

Emergency

Life-threatening symptoms. Call 911 / 999 / 112 or go to A&E immediately.
